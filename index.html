Bio

I am a fourth year Ph.D. student in the Stanford Computational Imaging Laboratory, advised by Prof. Gordon Wetzstein. My research interest is in 3D computer vision - specifically, I am interested in 3D-structured neural scene representations that allow machines to reason about our 3D world. I have previously worked on differentiable camera pipelines as well as VR and Human Perception.
